Finance and Banking Support

For small-scale loans related to agricultural implements, you can explore options through various banks and financial institutions. Check with your local banks for specific loan options and eligibility criteria.

Government Schemes Support

Here are some key Indian government schemes for farmers:

1. PM-Kisan: Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana
Website: https://pmkisan.gov.in

2. PMFBY: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana
Website: https://pmfby.gov.in

3. RKVY: Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana
Website: https://rkvy.nic.in

4. NABARD Schemes: National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development
Website: https://www.nabard.org

Please visit the respective websites for detailed information and application procedures.

Soil Testing Support

For soil testing related queries, you can reach out to your local agricultural extension office or soil testing laboratory. They can provide guidance on how to test your soil, interpret the results, and make recommendations for soil health improvement.
